BELIEFS / VALUES

Imagination is one of humankind's greatest gifts. For God's sake, USE IT. For good, I hope.

If you’re trying to “be authentic” you’re already not authentic.

I don’t care how intellectually intelligent you are. If you don’t have emotional intelligence and don’t treat people with kindness and respect, I cannot look up to you.

You can learn something from everybody, even a homeless person. I don’t believe in the phrase “they’re not worthy enough to talk to.”

Tell people you love them, even if the time is “never right.”

Gaslighting yourself is good. Like what do you mean I can’t build a company at 19, do gymnastics when I’m 90, win the Timothée Chalamet look-alike contest, become a dinosaur when I grow up, AND colonize uranus...🤨🤨🤨

The best leaders are people who's mission is to make other people leaders.

Good mental health. This sounds like a no-brainer, but it’s EMBARRASING how many people (including me) don’t prioritize this all the time.

If you have to choose between fear and regret, choose fear.

Be a girl's girl.
